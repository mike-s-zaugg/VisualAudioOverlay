# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

This project serves a disability community. Respect for accessibility needs and
for people with hearing loss is a core expectation, not an afterthought.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and its users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or sustained off-topic argument.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
  setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainer through https://github.com/mike-s-zaugg. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed
necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
